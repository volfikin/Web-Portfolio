MONTKOVO
30th Anniversary

Deliverables:

  • Brand Film
  • Recruitment Videos
  • Short-form Content
  • Cinematography
  • Post-Production

A 30th anniversary video package created for Montkovo, filmed across offices, production, and active construction sites. The project includes a hero anniversary film supported by a set of recruitment edits and short-form content for digital and social use.


30 years strong, stable, and moving forward. That’s the message the main film is built around, using a more cinematic and emotional approach.


Short-form output was focused in two ways. Informative, cinematic cutdown for brand presence and faster, more action oriented edits focusing on the energy in construction and recruitment appeal.
